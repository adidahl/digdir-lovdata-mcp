# Legal Data License

The MVP uses Lovdata `publicData` datasets for current laws and central
regulations. These datasets are planned as NLOD 2.0 open-data inputs.

Required attribution:

```text
Contains data under the Norwegian Licence for Open Government Data (NLOD 2.0)
made available by Stiftelsen Lovdata. The data has been processed, parsed, and
indexed by Digdir Norwegian Law MCP.
```

Every ingested record must keep its source URL, source dataset, archive
filename, archive freshness metadata, and raw XML hash.

Restricted datasets, including case-law text, preparatory works, and Lovdata
Pro-only material, are out of scope for the MVP.

This document is an engineering compliance record, not legal advice.
